About Us

Over 18 Years of Teaching Experience in STEM field.

Ashutosh Sir, an exceptionally driven individual with a commitment to perfection, holds a meticulous approach in his teaching. As a triple IIT alumnus, he takes great pride in guiding over 1000 students in various STEM fields with mentees successfully securing seats in 10+ esteemed IITs/NITs and other premier institutions nationwide. He advocates passionately for making science and mathematics enjoyable and accessible to students from diverse backgrounds.

Ashutosh Sir has dedicated himself to the philosophy of hands-on learning, turning traditionally perceived challenging subjects into engaging experiences. Beyond mere academic success, he strives for holistic growth in his students, nurturing both academic achievements and personal development.
